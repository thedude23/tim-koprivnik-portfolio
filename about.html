About me

I'm an open-minded ape in search of a peaceful, intentional, and unstressed life, who likes to learn new things, self-improve, read, watch docuseries, do sports, travel and meet unique people. Two things I cannot live without are chill music and the feeling of not being stuck. One of my idealistic life goals is to witness a world where money (or materialistic shit in general) wouldn't be the primary goal of it all and exploitation not an everyday human relation. Above all else, I try to be fair and empathetic, and I expect the same thing from anyone else. In the end, I'm like Newton's 3rd law -- I give what I get.
